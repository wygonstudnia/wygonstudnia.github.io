Learn regular expressions through play

Regulars is a game created to help you develop skills in using regular expressions. You learn best through first-hand experiences. Play-based learning does not focus too much on loading you with information. Instead, you have the opportunity to be active and involved in your learning. But don't take my word for it - try it yourself!
